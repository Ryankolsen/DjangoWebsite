About Ryan

Hi, I'm Ryan Olsen. Thanks for checking out my site and some of my projects.

After achieving my second Bachelor's degree majoring in Management Information Systems in 2013 I fell in love with software engineering. I started coding with Java and Java FX. Next I expanded my languages to include Python, JavaScript, JSON, HTML/CSS, SQL, C++ and anything else I can find.

Recently I discovered Jupiter Notebook and Tableau for Data Science projects. I was amazed by the sheer amount of data a Notebook can manage and organize. I am also constantly impressed by the functionality of Tableau.

I genuinely love breaking larger applications down in to small, manageable parts and working to find the most efficient and effective solution. I look forward to finding a career where I can be challenged and where I can continue to learn and grow as a software engineer.
